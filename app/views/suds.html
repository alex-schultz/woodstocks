Suds & Suds

With over 30 domestic, microbrews, and imports on tap, Suds & Suds Bar offers one of the widest selections of beer in Corvallis. Suds & Suds offers a friendly, laid back atmosphere where you can enjoy a beer, some pizza and watch your favorite team on the big screen TV. There is always a good mix of locals and college-aged patrons who enjoy the reasonably priced beer.

Suds & Suds also has some other amenities. If the sun is shining, you can go outside onto the Suds deck and enjoy the good weather. Come on into Suds & Suds, have a beer, and enjoy the company!
